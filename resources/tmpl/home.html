Redemption.

It's the central story of the Bible.

It’s the foundation of our life as community. It’s the substance of God’s promise to the world. And it’s found only in Jesus. We live in hope because “in Him we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins” (Eph 1:7). At Trinity Church, we exist to worship the God who is holy and merciful, to encourage each other as we shape our lives by the gospel, and to hold out the message of redemption in the heart of Nashville.

Sundays At Trinity

Consider Jesus: The Message of Hebrews

This year our sermons will consider the beautiful and enigmatic New Testament book of Hebrews. This book is a celebration of Jesus as the Son of God who became our perfect High Priest. It's an argument for why Jesus offers what no other savior can. And it's written with a profoundly pastoral purpose. It's an appeal to hold fast to Jesus even when the promises of a hope we can't see are called in question.
